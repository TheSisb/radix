---
title: Text
component: Text
description: A primitive useful for rendering text.
---

```js live
<Text>
  With Modulz, our goal is to fix the disconnect between digital product teams.
</Text>
```

### children

<Prop>ReactNode</Prop>

The content to render inside the Heading.

### size

<Prop isOptional default="2">
  0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8
</Prop>

There are 9 size options to choose from. Each size handles its own `font-size`, `letter-spacing` and `text-indent` accordingly.

```js live
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={0}>Text component</Text>
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={1}>Text component</Text>
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={2}>Text component</Text>
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={3}>Text component</Text>
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={4}>Text component</Text>
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={5}>Text component</Text>
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={6}>Text component</Text>
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={7}>Text component</Text>
<Text as="p" mt={1} size={8}>Text component</Text>
```

### weight

<Prop isOptional default="400">
  400 | 500
</Prop>

There are 2 weight options to choose from.

```js live
<Text fontWeight="400">Text component</Text>
<Text fontWeight="500">Text component</Text>
```

### truncate

<Prop isOptiona default="false">
  Boolean
</Prop>

When `true` this will truncate your text based on its maximum width.

```js live
<Box width={100}>
  <Text as="p" truncate>
    With Modulz, our goal is to fix the disconnect between digital product
    teams.
  </Text>
</Box>
```
